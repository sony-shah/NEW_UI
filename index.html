ABOUT US
The Best Choice for your Business.
The year 1999 witnessed the birth of a fledgeling IT company, Vidarbha Infotech Private Limited (VIPL) in Nagpur. Over the years, its promoter, Prashant Ugemuge, an Electronics Engineer ensured that the company never lost focus that is ever growing based on the most sound business principles and ethics. To cater the list of ever growing clients and their needs, VIPL became a multi-city organisation with offices in Mumbai, Pune & Nagpur. We provide complete IT solutions and provide new emerging technologies to increase the efficiency of the IT in any organization. Unmatched services and 100 % customer satisfaction defines our success over the past 23 years. As it is said that the service is the backbone of any organisation, VIPL has got a very firm belief in providing very professional service to its customers. With a dedicated team the endeavor is to delight the customer with the value added service. In this ongoing process of growth and development the company is achieving one milestone after another.
